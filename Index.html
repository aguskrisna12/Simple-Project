About

Early Life and Career

Martin Garrix was born on May 14, 1996, in Amstelveen, Netherlands. He developed an interest in music at a young age and started producing electronic music in his teenage years.p>

Youthful Influence:

His remarkable success at a young age has made him a role model for many aspiring DJs and producers who look up to him as an example of what can be achieved through dedication and talent.

Breakthrough with "Animals"

Garrix gained international fame and recognition with his hit single "Animals" in 2013. The track became a massive success and catapulted him to stardom, making him one of the youngest and most prominent DJs in the world.

Live Performances

Martin Garrix is a regular performer at major music festivals around the world, headlining events like Tomorrowland, Ultra Music Festival, and Coachella. His live shows are known for their high energy and captivating visuals.
